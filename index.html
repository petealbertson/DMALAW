Employment

We represent employees and firmer employees. When you have been wrongly died from your job or treated unfairly in violation of the law, we can help. We have assisted employees working at small local businesses and large national companies. Know your rights. Give us a call. Cases are handled on a contingency basis so you pay no fee unless you receive compensation. There is no charge for initial consultations.

Sexual Harassment

We represent employees who have been sexually harassed or forced to work in a hostile environment. If you have been sexually harassed or had to try to work in a hostile environment, call us. All cases are handled on a contingency basis so you pay no fee unless you receive compensation. Initial consultations are free.

Auto Accidents & Other Injuries

If you have been injured in an automobile or other accident, we are here for you. Insurance companies have experienced adjusters and lawyers representing them. For over 30 years we have helped to level the playing field on behalf of people who have been injured, we can let you know what to expect and what your rights are. Our representation is on a contingency basis so you pay no fee unless you receive compensation. Initial consultations are free.
